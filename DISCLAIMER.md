## Scope

This project applies survival analysis techniques — specifically Cox
Proportional Hazards and Accelerated Failure Time models — to publicly
available market data solely for **academic and educational purposes**.
The models, outputs, and visualisations are intended to demonstrate
methodological concepts from biostatistics and econometrics, not to
generate actionable trading signals.
